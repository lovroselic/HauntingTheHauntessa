About:

Haunting the Hauntessa is the thrilling sequel to Castle Haunt II (and I, of course), The Curse of the Castle Creep, and all its legendary predecessors. It draws inspiration from early 90s 3D games like Doom (1993) and Tomb Raider (1996), and carries on the saga of the Princess, brat turned badass heroine in high heels. It caters to retro aficionados who prefer playing on normal screens rather than tickling their phones.

HTH runs on the improved CastleHaunt II engine, lovingly called ENGINE, embracing full 3D gameplay, not just the look. ENGINE is heavily optimized for performance, with GLSL shaders that make your GPU nicely warm and cosy. It should run at 60 FPS on any modern computer, no, a 286 is not considered modern.
